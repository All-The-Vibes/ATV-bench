# Code of Conduct

ATV-Bench contributors must:

- discuss people and projects respectfully;
- critique evidence, methods, code, and claims rather than individuals;
- disclose benchmark and harness conflicts;
- protect private tasks, credentials, and security reports;
- avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, or targeted abuse;
- report benchmark defects honestly, even when they change rankings;
- never pressure maintainers to conceal, rewrite, or selectively rerun unfavorable evidence.

Maintainers may remove content, revoke access, reject submissions, or ban participation for conduct
that threatens contributor safety or benchmark integrity.

Report conduct issues privately to repository maintainers. Security-sensitive conduct should use the
security advisory channel described in `SECURITY.md`.
